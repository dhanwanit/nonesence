  • ANIMATION & VFX

    NANSENSE provides complete pipelines and professional-grade equipment for production houses animating computer generated characters for the film, television and video games industries. Over the years our full-body systems and gloves, working independently or in tandem with large optical stages, our systems have generated thousands of hours of content.

  • BIOMECHANICS & DATA ANALYSIS

    NANSENSE systems are capable of analyzing the biomechanics of human motion and lower body locomotion including walking, running and jumping. When paired with pressure sensing feet pads, the systems can also detect pressure exerted against the floor by each foot to determine weight distribution and the musculoskeletal axis of balance.

  • ERGONOMICS & DIGITAL FACTORY

    NANSENSE systems are efficient at capturing data in confined spaces without the need of specific lighting conditions or the fear of occlusion. R2 systems are used around the world in factory environments, some of which are magnetically challenged, to measure the ergonomics of new products or the biomechanics of factory workers working on assembling lines.

  • MEDICAL & SPORTS SCIENCE

    NANSENSE offers complete solutions aimed at medical and sports sciences. R2s are used for research in various fields including Parkinson’s research, idiopathic scoliosis research, treatment response monitoring, telemedicine and patient rehabilitation monitoring. R2s have an unmatched number of sensors that produce accurate full-body and fingers data
